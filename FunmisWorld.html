I am Olufunmilayo Dosunmu

I am so excited to share a piece of my World with you! A little bit about me:

- My name is Olufunmilayo Dosunmu but everyone calls me Funmi (Foo-me)

- Born and raised in New Jersey and a proud Nigerian-American

- Attended the Lawrenceville School (an independent boarding high-school)

- Undergraduate degree from Cornell University in Ithaca, New York, with a major in Asian Studies (and loved every second of it!)

- Worked for a few years in Operations and Logistics Management before heading to graduate school

- Moved to Chicago, IL in January 2015 to attend the University of Chicago, for a Master's in Computer Science

- Working as an Event Consultant (event planner) fueled by a passion for planning and entertaining

- Looking to leverage my knowledge of technology to enhance operations, logistics and design in different aspects of product management

Product Management
